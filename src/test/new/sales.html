Agency

We have a strong network of Financial Consultants - also known as insurance agents - who market our insurance products and plans. They work out of home or the office and are instrumental in helping customers take the first step in insuring themselves and their families.

Bancassurance

Through the Bancassurance channel, we distribute retail and individual insurance solutions and products through tie-ups with banks, NBFCs, MFIs and SFBs. Other HDFC Group companies like HSL, HDFC, and HDFC are part of this channel as well. Over the years, we have grown and evolved developing relationships with key banks like RBL Bank, Saraswat Co-operative Bank, IDFC First Bank among others.

Direct Sales

Direct sales channels give you direct access to customers without any intermediaries. The different modes of distribution in this channel include:

Loyalty - where leads are provided by outbound and inbound contact centres and the portfolio of customers are classified based on different campaigns.

Branch Customer Service & Sales (BCSS) caters to customer walk-ins at HDFC Life branches.

Direct Service Channel (DSC) taps into existing customers and by effectively resolving customer queries upsells to them.

Ecommerce and
Digital Marketing

In an agile world, a successful online presence is paramount to getting leads, building trust and converting prospects to customers. Group Sales

You will recommend and enable retirement solutions to corporate clients for their Gratuity/ Superannuation/Leave Encashment plans.

The principle lines of business include:

Employee Benefit Solutions through Funds Business

Employee Protection Plans through Group Term Insurance Business

Development Insurance Business for the Social Sector
