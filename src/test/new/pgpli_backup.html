Got questions? Get the right answers.

  • 01
    What is the PGPLI program?

    It is an 11-month Postgraduate Program in Life Insurance (PGPLI) brought to in association with upGrad. This program gears you towards a career in insurance and trains you on the fundamentals of Life Insurance, financial services, products, and sales management. It prepares you for a rewarding Front-line Sales job at HDFC Life.

  • 02
    Why is it unique?

    The program offers a guaranteed job with HDFC Life in Sales, with a starting salary of INR. 2,50,000* per annum plus incentives** on successful completion of the course.
    *All payments will be subject to prevailing tax laws and statutory deductions
    **Subject to performance and available schemes

  • 03
    What are the benefits of a partnership with upGrad?

    upGrad is one of the most respected brands in the country and has trained over 3,00,000 professionals across industries and have been instrumental in their professional growth. upGrad works with top Indian and Global universities such as IIIT Bangalore, MICA, Cambridge Judge Business School, London University, BITS Pilani and many more.

  • 04
    What is the duration of the program?

    It is a 11-month program structured as follows:
    Phase 1- Two months of online training with no monthly allowance.
    Phase 2 - Two months on-the-job training (OJT) at any HDFC Life branch with a monthly stipend of INR. 2,500*/-.
    Phase 3 – Seven months internship at any HDFC Life branch with a monthly stipend of INR 15,100*/-.
    *All payments will be subject to prevailing tax laws and other statutory deductions

  • 05
    Who is eligible to apply for this course?

    The minimum requirement is a graduate degree. Students in their final year of college awaiting graduation result may also apply. We expect you to have graduated before moving into the On-the-job phase of the program. You will be expected to produce your official transcripts and degree certificate at this time.
    If you do not graduate on time, the provisional offer will be cancelled.

  • 06
    What are the program fees?

    The course fee is INR 99,000/- (inclusive of all taxes) payable to upGrad.

  • 07
    Are the fees refundable?

    The fees are charged solely by upGrad for imparting training and is non-refundable. All terms and conditions for payment of fees are governed by upGrad’s rules and refund policy.
    Note: HDFC Life does not collect any fee (even on behalf of upGrad) and shall not be liable for any refund of the course fee.

  • 08
    What documents will I need to enrol for the program?

    You would have to submit the copies of the following documents to be eligible for the program:
    Degree certificate
    Official college transcripts
    Resume
    Passport-size photographs
    PAN card and Aadhar (mandatory)
    Relieving letter from your employer (if employed in the past or present)

  • 09
    What is the selection process?

    There are three stages to the selection process:


    Step 1: Your profile will be shortlisted by HDFC Life based on your qualification, interest and potential for Sales. Proficiency in English also counts.

    Step 2: An online cognitive ability and online communication assessment will be administered by HDFC Life. This test will be virtually proctored through video-taping. If you clear this stage, then you will advance to step 3.

    Step 3: Interview by HDFC Life to validate verbal communication and other critical skills required for the job on offer.

    This will conclude in your being selected or rejected for the course.

  • 10
    Will I have to complete a project as part of the program?

    Classroom assignments and continuous assessments are part of the curriculum. In addition, you will receive on-the-job training and several opportunities to explore real-world work scenarios. In lieu of a thesis, there is an internship you will undergo to graduate.

  • 11
    Where are the classes held and when will they begin?

    The training is held over two months and is completely online. You can study in the comfort of your home or any place else. No stipend is paid during this period.

  • 12
    Where will on-the-job training be conducted? Will I be charged for it?

    You will undergo a two-month OJT at a HDFC Life branch or partner offices closest to you.The location will be assigned to you after you complete the online training and you will be paid a monthly stipend of INR. 2500*. *All payments will be subject to prevailing tax laws and other statutory deductions

  • 13
    Are internships part of the program?

    Yes, internships are integral to the program. You will undergo a seven-month internship at HDFC Life office closest to you and earn a monthly
    stipend of INR. 15,100/-*. You will also receive incentives for achieving business targets as per the prevailing incentive scheme (if applicable).
    *All payments will be subject to prevailing tax laws and other statutory deductions

  • 14
    Does this program guarantee a job?

    When you complete the course successfully, you are guaranteed a full-time job at HDFC Life in Sales with a starting salary of INR 2,50,000 p.a.* plus incentives. There are no pre-conditions, except that you successfully complete the course. A successful completion of the training would mean timely completion of all assignments, quizzes, assessments during training, minimum attendance, and meeting assigned targets during OJT and internship.
    *All payments will be subject to prevailing tax laws and other statutory deductions.

  • 15
    Who issues my employment letter?

    HDFC Life will provide you a provisional offer letter that stands confirmed after you complete all phases of the training successfully. The offer letter clearly mentions the terms and conditions of employment, including applicable statutory and tax deductions.
