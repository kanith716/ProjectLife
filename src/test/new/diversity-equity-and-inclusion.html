Women @ HDFC Life

HDFC Life celebrates the contribution of its women employees to the organization. We are committed to creating an empowering workplace for our women staff and have taken several focused steps to nurture such a culture.

  • Punarāgaman: This initiative is designed to jump start women’s second innings at work and ensure seamless transition into corporate world by providing varied flexible working arrangements.
  • Know our offerings
  • Maternity Transition Program: Our structured Maternity Program is designed to facilitate smooth transition into motherhood through pregnancy, surrogacy and adoption. A 24*7 care helpline service helps to deal with concerns related to maternity, performance, anxiety, work life balance etc.
  • Women Mentorship : We also have an exclusive mentorship program for women, to leverage the power of in-house communities and build a strong women leadership pipeline at the mid and senior levels.
  • Exclusive policies to support women: Our policies, procedures and ecosystem all work to create an inclusive culture for the women employees. There are well defined policies depicting zero tolerance against discrimination, bullying, harassment and victimization on any ground, including age, race, ethnic or national origin, color, mental or physical health conditions, disability, pregnancy, gender, gender expression, gender identity, sexual orientation, marital status or other domestic circumstances, employment status, working hours or other flexible working arrangements, or religion or belief

Pride @ HDFC Life

HDFC Life celebrates uniqueness of every individual irrespective of their gender preference.

  • To ensure the LGBTQ+ community members feel safe and included, the company has a set of well defined processes towards zero tolerance against discrimination, bullying, harassment and victimization on any ground, including gender, gender expression, gender identity or sexual orientation.
  • An exclusive counselling service has been set up to assist in mental well being of the LGBTQ+ community.
  • Gender Re-assignment policy assists employees who wish to undergo the gender re-assignment process.
  • The company also recognizes same-gender partner nomination in its mediclaim policy.
  • The dress code at HDFC Life is gender-neutral. Employees can choose to express themselves as they like while adhering to the gender-neutral formal and causal attires described by the company.
